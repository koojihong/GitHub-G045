Section 1

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

Expense DataAmount, category, date, descriptionTo power your dashboard and reports

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Budget Bee service
  • Authenticate you when you sign in
  • Display your expense history, budget status, and savings progress
  • Generate dashboard charts and financial summaries
  • Send password reset emails when you request them
✓ We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the Service to you.
Section 3

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured SQLite database. Security measures include:

  • Password hashing — passwords are hashed with SHA-256 and never stored in plain text
  • Session security — sessions use signed cookies and expire on logout
  • Password reset tokens — tokens are unique, expire after 1 hour, and are single-use
⚠ While we implement industry-standard security practices, no system is 100% secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your account.
Section 4

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Budget Bee users
  • With your explicit written consent
Section 5

Cookies

Budget Bee uses one cookie: a session cookie to keep you signed in. This cookie:

  • Is set only when you sign in
  • Does not track your browsing across other websites
  • Is cleared when you sign out or close your browser

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Correction — edit or update your information through the app
  • Deletion — delete individual records or your entire account

To exercise any of these rights, you can use the account settings in the app or contact us directly at sunyeeyong31@gmail.com.
